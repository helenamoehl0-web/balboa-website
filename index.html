Our Story

Born on Balboa Street.
Made for every table.

Lily Chan grew up on Balboa Street in San Francisco, in a household where her Vietnamese parents cooked every single day. Satay, lemongrass, fresh coriander — the kitchen was always alive. That's where she learned that food is more than fuel. It's how you say you belong somewhere.

Years later, she's living in Helsinki with her Finnish husband. She's vegan, she's far from home, and she misses those flavors — not just for herself, but to pass on. To her kids. To her dinner table. To anyone who's ever tasted something and felt, for a moment, completely at ease.

So she started blending. Drawing on Vietnamese seasoning traditions, the clean restraint of Scandinavian cooking, and plant-based protein as a foundation — five blends that carry a little bit of everywhere she's from.

  • Dill Ranch — the Nordic freshness of her new home
  • Spicy Satay Chili — her mother's kitchen, in a jar
  • Oregano Flow — Mediterranean evenings with friends
  • Banh Mi — Vietnam, Balboa Street, the taste of home
  • Seaweed Drift — the Baltic Sea before Helsinki, umami-deep
