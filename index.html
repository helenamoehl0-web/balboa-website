Our Story

Born on Balboa Street.
Made for every table.

Lily Chan grew up in San Francisco — on Balboa Street. Her Vietnamese parents cooked every day. The smell of satay, lemongrass, fresh coriander. She learned: food is home. Food is love.

Years later, she lives with her Finnish husband in Helsinki. She's vegan. She misses the flavors of her childhood — and wants to pass them on. To her children. To her table. To everyone who understands that good food is more than nutrition.

So she started blending. Vietnamese seasoning tradition, Scandinavian purity, plant-based protein as a base.

  • Dill Ranch — the Nordic freshness of her new home
  • Spicy Satay Chili — her mother's kitchen, in a jar
  • Oregano Flow — Mediterranean evenings with friends
  • Banh Mi — Vietnam, Balboa Street, the taste of home
  • Seaweed Drift — the Baltic Sea before Helsinki, umami-deep
